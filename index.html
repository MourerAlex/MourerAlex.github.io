Thinking carefully about causation, not just association.

This site explores modern methods in statistics, biostatistics, causal inference, epidemiology and machine learning — with two goals: distil complex methods into accessible explanations, and bridge theory and practice by implementing ideas from recent research in practical settings.

Causal Inference Biostatistics Machine Learning Survival Analysis

Posts aim to keep the target of inference scientifically meaningful and assumptions explicit. A particular focus on longitudinal data, time-to-event outcomes, and applications in clinical medicine and pharmacoepidemiology.

Everything here is written to be rigorous but readable — equations when necessary, intuition always.
